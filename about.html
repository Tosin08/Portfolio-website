My work process

Research

You can't solve a problem without understanding your user's need. Before I start off designing, I ask a lot of questions to understand the problems my clients wants to solve, as well as understanding potential users & their challenges. This Phase usually involves User research, Competitive analysis, consulting with experts & ideation on possible solutions.

Design

Once problems have been defined, I start out designing by creating First-draft designs, which goes from sketches to wireframes, mockups & prototypes. This process results into designing touchpoints that are intuitive for the users & good for the business.

Testing & iteration

This Involves conducting user testing with an appropriate audience to evaluate product with user experience, then making applicable changes based on gathered feedback.

Coordination

Communicating & Working with both front-end & back-end developers to create proper functionality
